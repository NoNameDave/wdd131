Why Houseplants?

Houseplants improve air quality, reduce stress, and make your space feel alive.

Snake Plant

Tolerates low light and infrequent watering. Perfect for beginners!

Pothos

Fast-growing vine that thrives in a variety of lighting conditions.

Peace Lily

Elegant and forgiving. Prefers low light and moist soil.

Spider Plant

Easy to grow and propagate. Loves indirect light and occasional watering.

Calathea

Calatheas enjoy medium to low indirect light and thrive in high humidity. Keep their soil consistently moist.
