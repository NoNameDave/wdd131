Watering

Most houseplants prefer evenly moist soil. Let the top inch dry before watering again.

Lighting

Low-light plants like Snake Plants, while succulents need bright indirect sunlight.

Humidity

Bathrooms are ideal for moisture-loving plants like ferns and calatheas.

Soil

Use well-draining potting mix and containers with drainage holes.
